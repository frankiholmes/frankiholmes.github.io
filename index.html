Elite Wolfpack Philosophy and Purpose:

We are dedicated to providing a healthy atmosphere and positive attitude for your child. Each of our highly motivated and well-trained coaches helps each student to develop gymnastics skills to reach his/her potential and goals. The program is designed to improve coordination, physical ability, motivation, and self-image. We stress respect for the coaches and fellow students and the coaches show equal respect for the gymnasts. Our open-door policy allows parents to make suggestions and recommendations. We value everyone's input as we strive to work as a team - staff, students, and parents.

You will find Elite's Gymnastics facilities are second to none. We offer over 12,000 square feet of gymnastic and recreational facilities. Our gym features a recessed floor with wall to wall padding & carpeting. Inside you will also find only safe state of the art olympic equipment including an inground trampoline.

Meet the Coaches

Johnny Moyal, heads the Elite Wolfpack and offers the knowledge and experience of a champion. He is a 3 time Olympian and a 3 time SEC Champion for Louisiana State University with All American and All SEC honors. Johnny was also a member of 5 World Championship Teams.

Franki Holmes, former JO athlete and a former student of Moyal works alongside with him. She currently attends LSU while helping Moyal develop the program. Holmes is not only a former high level athlete, but also a current NAWGJ gymnastics judge for the state of Louisiana.
